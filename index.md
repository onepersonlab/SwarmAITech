<section class="page-content" style="background: var(--color-bg-secondary); padding-top: var(--space-4xl);">
  <div class="container">
    <header class="section-header">
      <h2 class="section-title">What is Multi-Agent AI?</h2>
    </header>
    
    <div class="page-body" style="max-width: 800px;">
      <p>Multi-agent AI systems represent a paradigm shift in artificial intelligence, where multiple autonomous agents collaborate to solve complex problems. Unlike traditional single-agent approaches, multi-agent frameworks enable:</p>
      
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Task Decomposition</strong> — Breaking complex tasks into manageable subtasks distributed among specialized agents</li>
        <li><strong>Collaborative Intelligence</strong> — Agents working together, sharing information and coordinating actions</li>
        <li><strong>Role-Based Architecture</strong> — Different agents taking on specific roles (researcher, coder, reviewer, etc.)</li>
        <li><strong>Self-Improvement</strong> — Systems that learn and evolve through agent interactions</li>
      </ul>
      
      <h3>Key Applications</h3>
      <p>Multi-agent frameworks are being used across various domains:</p>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Scientific Research</strong> — Automating literature review, hypothesis generation, and paper writing</li>
        <li><strong>Software Development</strong> — Collaborative coding, testing, and deployment</li>
        <li><strong>Business Operations</strong> — Task orchestration, decision support, and workflow automation</li>
        <li><strong>Personal Assistants</strong> — Comprehensive AI assistants that can handle complex, multi-step requests</li>
      </ul>
